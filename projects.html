Project: Space Platformers
Course: Computer Science Principles
Date: August 30, 2018 - September 6, 2018
Course: Computer Science Principles

The objective of the Scratch Project was to design a game or an animation using the code blocks. Anirudh and I chose to make a game called Space Platformers, where you try to get the character to the end-point while overcoming any of the obstacles the level has for you. My role in the project was the documenter, so I would document everything that my partner Anirudh coded. In my documentation, I included a flowchart, a daily work log, and a feedback table. I learned that it is important to communicate with your partner or co-worker, or otherwise you will not have the final product that both of you envisioned. I also learned that it is important to share, as without Anirudh's help, I would have never finished the documentation, and without my help, Anirudh would have never finished coding the game. This was our first step in our programming careers.
